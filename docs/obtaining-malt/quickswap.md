# Using quickswap

1. Go to [https://quickswap.exchange/\#/swap](https://quickswap.exchange/#/swap) and Connect your wallet

   ![assets/Screenshot\_2021-06-05\_at\_11.54.18.png](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_2021-06-05_at_11.54.18.png)

2. Select the required From - To coin pair in the swap window, enter the amounts and click Swap.

   ![assets/Screenshot\_2021-06-05\_at\_11.56.10.png](../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_2021-06-05_at_11.56.10.png)
